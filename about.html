About Me

I'm a solutions architect with more than 4 years of experience in application and solutions architecture. I design and build information systems tailored to your needs, environment, and regulations.

What I do

Information Systems Design

I collect requirements and use modern methodologies such as domain-driven design to identify architecture building blocks. Use enterprise integration patterns to integrate systems. Make sure your applications and environment are secure and meet your standards.

System & Data Migration

Whether you are migrating from a legacy system to a newer one or from on-premises to cloud. I design your migration strategy and help you minimize the risks accompanied by the process.

Cloud Architecture

I build your cloud strategy and help you make the best use of available services. I help you optimize your cost, secure your environment and meet regulatory and compliance requirements.

Architecture Governance

I ensure your architecture principles and processes are implemented and keep your architecture repository updated. I manage the lifecycle of architecture components and support the assessment and selection of products and technologies.
