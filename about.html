Hello, I'm Omar

I'm a solutions architect specializing in Application and Cloud Architecture.

I solve your business problems by breaking them down and providing realistic solutions suitable for your needs and tuned to your unique environment.

What I do

Architecture Repository Management

Manage Applications portfolio & Landscape. Track and plan Application Lifecycle. Map applications with business processes.

Architecture Gap Analysis

Identify gaps and potential solutions. Conduct market research. Lead RFIs, RFP writing, and product evaluation.

Design Your Application Architecture

Use modern methods such as DDD to identify architecture blocks and design mini and microservices.

Design Integration Architecture

Design Your API Management Strategy. Use modern enterprise integration patterns and technologies: BPMN, DMN, AMQP, ESB.

Application Security

Make sure your architecture is secure by ensuring security requirements are properly designed and implemented. Incorporate IAM, Encryption, Digital Signature, auditing, and event monitoring into your architecture.

Automation & DevOps

Design and introduce automation and DevOps components to your architecture for faster development and delivery.

Design & Document Your Data Models

Design data models at different levels: Conceptual, Logical, and Physical. Classify & Label your data based on your policy and help you secure your data accordingly.

Design Data Integration

Design data migration, transformation, and preparation using ETL/ELT and other data integration patterns.

Cloud & Technology Architecture

Design deployment architecture. Estimate and optimize hardware sizing. Design your cloud strategy and architecture.

Governance, Risk and Compliance

Make sure your architecture is compliant and able to adapt to changes to internal and external regulations such as SAMA CSF and PCI-DSS.
